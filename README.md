# Warden

> *Stone remembers the chisel. The listening stone wakes.*

---

You carry a letter. Worn. Read many times. Written in a hand you would know anywhere.

*Come home when you can. Come home safe.*

You are a Warden — an iron gate between the mortal world and the Void pressing against it. The Leyline does not give; it only lends. Every spell you cast is borrowed power, and the debt is paid in blood, focus, and fracture. Cast too many times and your veins darken, your vision blurs, your hands shake. Cast one more and the vessel breaks.

**Warden** is a dark fantasy text RPG powered by a live language model that knows the old ways — HEMA sword fighting doctrine, elemental immunities, the economy of four rival realms, and thirty-two Words of power arranged in circles of ascending cost.

The Chronicle does not forgive. Neither does the Void.

---

## What It Actually Feels Like

> *The Chronicle opens. The ink is wet.*

You're not clicking through dialogue trees. You're reading a chronicle being written as you act.

Draw a sword and the Chronicle describes the screech of steel, the vibration of a bind through the hilt. Cast *Ig Fac* (Spark) into a Grave Walker and it does nothing — iron cannot wound it, and lesser magic fails — because the Chronicle consults its Bestiary and plays it straight. Speak the wrong Word in the wrong fight and pay for it. Let your Fracture climb to four and every spell deals twenty damage back to you.

The world has four regions — Valdris, Aeloria, Thornhaven, the Pale Reaches — each with its own ambient and combat music that shifts when you cross borders or draw blood. The Grimoire tracks which of the thirty-two Words you've learned. The Stone (a local vector memory model) etches every action and consults that history during combat.

And somewhere in your inventory is a letter from Lyssa. It cannot be lost, sold, or destroyed. It simply is.

## The Thirty-Two Words

Magic is organized into four Circles of ascending power and cost. You begin knowing the First Circle. The Fourth Circle is never sold — only found.

| Circle | Examples |
|---|---|
| **I — Foundation** | Ad Vita (Heal), Ig Fac (Spark), Ful Min (Shock), Frig Tel (Ice Arrow) |
| **II — Combat** | Jact Ign (Fireball), Mag Ful (Lightning), San Luc (Purify), Neg Mag (Dispel) |
| **III — Utility** | Mor Loq (Speak with Dead), Terr Mens (Fear), Pel Dur (Stone Skin) |
| **IV — Mastery** | Luc Div (Divine Light), Pel Ext (Banish), Terr Mot (Earthquake) |

Every cast adds to your Fracture. At three, frost burns your skin. At four, every spell costs twenty HP. At five, the vessel collapses.

## The Bestiary

The Chronicle consults the Bestiary on every strike. It does not forgive tactical errors.

**Iron cannot wound Voidlings.** Lesser magic fails. You must anchor them to reality with their opposing element — Fire against Ice Voidlings, Lightning against Stone Voidlings, Light against Shadows. Once anchored, iron bites.

**The Risen do not feel pain.** A Grave Walker walks through a shallow cut to land a heavy blow. Lightning cooks it inside its own armor.

**Vampires Double.** If the Noble Vampire is parried, it instantly changes the angle of the blade. It remembers mortality and weaponizes that memory.

**The Fallen are you, corrupted.** A Dark Warden knows all thirty-two Words and uses *Neg Mag* to strip your defenses before striking from the high guard. It cannot be exhausted. It forces you into Fracture.

## The Four Realms

| Realm | Character |
|---|---|
| **Valdris** | Military meritocracy. Honor-bound. Weapons cost 20% less. |
| **Aeloria** | Merchant republic. Pragmatic. Scrolls are commonly available. |
| **Thornhaven** | Isolationist. Deep forests. Old magic. Herbs at half price. |
| **The Pale Reaches** | Frontier territory. Desperate. No scrolls. No mercy. |

Each realm has its own ambient and combat music pool, cycling through tracks as you travel and fight.

## The Alchemical Circle

Three recipes. Craft them if you have the ingredients.

**Healing Salve** — Bloodroot + Lavender + Vial. Closes wounds.

**Potion of Night's Sight** — Wolfsbane + Bloodroot + Vial. See clearly in total darkness.

**Leyline Amplifier** — Silverleaf + Nightshade + Vial. Boosts spell potency temporarily.
